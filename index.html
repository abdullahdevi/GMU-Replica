About George Mason University

George Mason University is a leading educational institution known for its academic excellence and vibrant campus life. Established in 1972, we have a long history of nurturing talents and producing successful graduates.

We offer transformative education that enriches your life and positions you for career success. Here, you can reach your potential in business, health care, social work, and beyond, benefiting from the tremendous flexibility of our online programs. George Mason educates ambitious students across the U.S. and the globe, with multiple campuses in Virginia and South Korea.
